The Mt. Fuji region is now under the COVID-19 state of emergency

Due to the increasing cases of Covid-19, Shizuoka and Yamanashi prefectures have come under the state of emergency (scheduled until Sep. 12), and request not to vistit these regions, including Mt. Fuji, unless necessary.

Heeding points when climbing the Yoshida Trail of Mt.Fuji
  1. Please heed the following points when climbing Mt.Fuji
  2. Please do not walk off the mountain trail.It may cause a risk of slip down, and may induce falling rocks.
  3. Please wear a helmet while climbing.You can lent at 6th station Safety Guidance Center.
All major routes on Mt. Fuji are open

As of July 10, All major trails to the summit (Yoshida, Subashiri, Gotenba, Fujinomiya Trails) and ohachi-meguri (summit crator loop) are open, and accessible to the peak from any route.
